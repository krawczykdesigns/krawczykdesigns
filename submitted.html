About

David Krawczyk is an industrial designer based in New York City. He takes interest in the design of every day objects. Side projects often include repurposing products to save them from the landfill. Previously working at a consumer electronics design consultancy in Taipei, Taiwan, he has a working knowledge of design for mass manufacture. He holds a degree in Industrial Design from Pratt Institute.
